# PRODUCT.md, Lilia Belz, Soul Portraits

register: brand

## Product Purpose
A single-page portfolio and landing page for Lilia Belz, a portrait photographer.
The page is shared one to one: after a personal encounter, Lilia sends the link so a
prospective sitter can feel what a "Soul Portrait" session is about, then reach out.
The design is the product. Its job is to convey calm, trust, and craft, and to make
people want to be seen.

## Offering
Soul Portraits, process focused portrait sessions. The value is the encounter and the
experience of being truly seen, not just the resulting files. People leave feeling more
free and more themselves. Sessions are custom and personal: roughly one day a week with
room for two to four sittings, arranged individually.

## Users
People Lilia meets in person and invites to look. Often not "photo people". They are
curious, a little vulnerable, looking for an honest image of themselves. They decide
emotionally, on tone and trust, not on feature lists or price tables.

## Tone
Feinfuehlig, ehrlich, authentisch, ruhig, edel, schlicht, warm, intim. German language.
Quiet confidence over salesmanship. Invitation over pressure.

## Anti-references
- Loud wedding photographer template with sliders, badges, and pricing tables.
- Cold fashion minimalism that feels like a model agency.
- Generic SaaS landing patterns: hero metric blocks, identical icon cards, gradient text.
- Stocky, over-bright, over-saturated look.

## Strategic principles
- One clear action: get in touch to arrange an encounter. No hard pricing.
- Show range and humanity in the portfolio, diverse faces, real emotion.
- Mobile and desktop equally considered, the link is opened on a phone first.
- Editorial, gallery like restraint. Whitespace, serif display, warm paper light theme.
